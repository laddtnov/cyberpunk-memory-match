MISSION BRIEFING

> OBJECTIVE

Find all matching pairs of cyber operatives hidden behind the cards. Clear the board to complete your mission.

> HOW TO PLAY

  • Click a card to reveal the operative
  • Click a second card to find its match
  • Matched pairs stay revealed
  • Mismatched cards flip back after 1s
  • Match all pairs to complete the mission
